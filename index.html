About Me

I'm a product builder, developer, and team leader. Today, my focus is on building and empowering healthy cross-functional product teams that obsess over outcome and not output.

In the last three years I've been helping Earth Class Mail, virtual mailbox service, to realize its product potential. As VP of Engineering and Product, and currently CTO, grew the team from four engineers to twenty including UX designers, product managers, and data scientists. Consistently contributed to business strategy, product roadmaping, sales enablement. Based on both qualitative and quantitaive research, rebuilt aging user interface and introduced native iOS app Team's effort and dedication were accompannied by consistent revenue growth year over year, and highest user satisfaction Earth Class Mail ever recorded. And recenetly completed SOC audit proved that rapid growth, and product iteration can be achieved while maintaning high IT security methodoligies and standards.

When not helping business automate their postal mail I ride bikes (foot or motor powered) with my wife, ski, read about SaaS, build Magna-tile spaceships with my three-year-old, and look for things I can "fix" around the house.
