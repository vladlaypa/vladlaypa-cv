About me

With a decade of diverse experience building software, business acumen, and passion to understand customer needs, I lead and shape cross-functional product teams that obsess over outcome and not output.

Over the last three years I've been helping Earth Class Mail, a virtual mailbox service, realize its potential through category and product design, continuous experimentation, tech modernization, and growth hacking. As a CTO, I have been guiding the team that grew from four engineers to twenty including UX designers, product managers, data scientists, and developers. Currently, Earth Class Mail shows record numbers in activation, adoption, engagement, customer satisfaction and enterprise customer acquisition.

When not helping businesses digitize their postal mail, I enjoy bike riding (foot or motor powered) with my wife, skiing, reading about SaaS, building Magna-tile spaceships with my three-year-old son, and looking for things I can "fix" around the house.
